01 About

I work across the full lifecycle of AI-powered systems — designing architectures, engineering prompts, building retrieval pipelines on vector databases, evaluating model behavior, and deploying the result as something a user can actually rely on.

Most of my recent work centers on agentic systems: multi-step reasoning chains, tool-using agents, and orchestration graphs that coordinate multiple models toward a goal. I build these with LangChain and LangGraph, trace and debug them with LangSmith, and wire them into real workflows using n8n for agentic automation.

I'm currently focused on building scalable AI applications and intelligent agents that solve real-world business problems — not just proofs of concept.
